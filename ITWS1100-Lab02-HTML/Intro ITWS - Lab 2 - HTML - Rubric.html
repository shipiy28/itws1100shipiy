Resume Lab

The HTML5 Resume has ten possible points allotted as follows:

Topic Possible Points Description
Validity 2 Does the page validate?
2 points = completely valid
1 point = two or fewer errors (of different types)
0 points = more than two errors (of different types)
Indentation 1 Quality/legibility of indentation
Completeness 5 Is the resume done? Are all the major parts represented?
Quality 2 How well does the resume match the example? How is the overall code quality? How creative is the resume?
